An interview proxy service is a support method where an experienced industry expert sits for your live tech interview. By utilizing remote connection software and discrete audio configuration, the expert solves coding challenges, explains system architectures, and answers questions in real-time, ensuring a successful hire outcome.

Our standard pricing is $130 per individual proxy session. This covers remote desktop preparation, hardware coordination, matching you with a tailored technical interviewer, and executing the live interview. Group packages or high-frequency loops can be discussed with our coordinators.

We cover over 50+ technology stacks across SDE, Backend (Java, Go, Node, Python, C++), Frontend (React, Angular, Vue), Mobile (Swift, Kotlin, Flutter), QA (Automation, Selenium), Data Engineering (Spark, Hadoop, SQL), DevOps (AWS, Kubernetes, Terraform), AI/ML, ERP (SAP, Salesforce), and Cybersecurity. If your technology is niche, contact us and we will match you with a specialized expert.

Yes, confidentiality is our highest priority. All communication, payment details, candidate information, and setup logs are fully encrypted and deleted after the loop. No external profiles or names are ever linked or recorded.
